WELCOME AT AGHAKHAN DENTAL CLINIC



Welcome to Aghakhan Dental. In 1946, our father, Samuel Aronoff, founded this dental practice in SoHo, New York. For more than five decades, our dentists have maintained a firm commitment to providing quality dental care tailored to the needs of the individual patient. We offer everything from routine teeth cleaning and treatment for a toothache to advanced dental procedures for our patients in NYC. Whether you need a general dentist, restoration dentistry, or an Invisalign dentist, we provide a wide array of services to meet your dental health needs. Our services include teeth cleaning, dental fillings, root canal, TMJ treatment and sleep apnea treatment. If you want to improve your smile, we provide cosmetic services, such as teeth whitening, dental implants, Veneers, and Invisalign, as well as restoration dentistry, and treatment of periodontal disease. Our dentists and staff remain up to date with all advances and emerging technologies in our field. We utilize state of the art technology for accurate diagnosis and effective treatment for a broad range of dental problems. Our office is equipped with intraoral cameras, digital radiography, VELscope, Diagnodent laser cavity detector, and soft tissue laser technology to ensure superior care for our patients in NYC. Our website contains additional information about our dentists and the services provided at our dental office in SoHo, New York. Please browse our website to find information and new patient forms, view before and after pictures in our Gallery, and learn about various dental treatments. If you have questions or want to schedule an appointment, please contact our office at 212-226-5777.
